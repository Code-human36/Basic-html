Most Good looking Anime Characters

One of anime’s highlights has to be all the good looking guys. Many anime contain at least one male character so handsome that we all end up swooning. And that’s why we’ll be talking about the 15 best anime with good looking guys.

For this list, we’ll be considering those anime which have an ensemble cast of good looking guys, and not just one incredibly hot guy in an otherwise average or female-populated cast.

So, even though Usui comes to mind when thinking about handsome anime boys, Maid Sama! won’t appear in this list. And we must also add that the entries on this list are placed in no specific order.

So, without further ado, let’s begin the list of the 15 best anime with good looking guys.

  1. Free! - Iwatobi Swim Club

    Free! is a popular sports anime focusing on swimming. The story follows the protagonist, Haruka Nanase or Haru, and his friends at Iwatobi Swim Club as well as his old friend and rival, Rin Matsuoka.

    Free!

  2. Yona of the Dawn

    An amazing and underrated anime, Yona of the Dawn shows innocent and pampered princess Yona’s life falling apart one night when the guy she loves betrays her father and kills him. Yona sets out with her childhood friend Hak to find the Four Dragons to save her kingdom.

    Yona of the Dawn

  3. IDOLiSH7

    IDOLiSH7 is a game made into an anime, but unlike other such anime, this one has a great cast and a corrigible storyline that makes it a fan-favorite.

    IDOLiSH7

  4. Given

    BL anime often gets a stereotype attached to it which Given breaks wonderfully with its powerful and emotional story. It’s a story about Mafuyu, a teenager dealing with the suicide of his boyfriend, and his journey of love and healing through music.

    Given

  5. Jujutsu Kaisens Gojo

    Mainstream action anime contain a huge number of characters, many of whom are good looking. But the number of such anime are so many that we’ve excluded them from the list.

    Jujutsu Kaisen, however, snatches this spot with drop-dead gorgeous characters Gojo, Sukuna, Geto, Nanami and many more. With an amazing story and brilliant action, this is one of the best anime with good looking guys.

    Gojo Satoru
